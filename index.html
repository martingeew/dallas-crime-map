Dallas Crime Map 2024

Interactive visualization of Dallas police incidents with focus on property crimes and burglary incidents for 2024.

This project analyzes Dallas Police Department incident data to create an interactive web-based map showing the geographic distribution of property crimes across different zip codes in Dallas.
